Bringing you all sides of the story.

Praelia will be a Database focused on the Ancient World.

Myths were passed down by story. This means that lots of variation has occured.

So, we will find variations of popular myths and determine both how they were varied and how reliable they are.



What to expect:

Myths retold by different authors of the ancient world.

A library of primary sources ranging from illustrations to artifacts.

Links to other websites and databases for more information.

Pages updated and added weekly.



Currently a work-in-progress!

We are working hard everyday to develop and expand Praelia.

We are projected to be finished by 2026.
